About the College

Building Tomorrow's Tech Leaders Today

Our Story

Founded in 2011, the College of Computer Studies has been at the forefront of technological education and innovation. We are dedicated to developing competent, ethical, and innovative computer professionals who will contribute to the advancement of technology and society.

Our commitment to excellence has made us one of the leading institutions in computer education, recognized both nationally and internationally for our impact in the field.

Our Mission

To develop competent, ethical, and innovative computer professionals who will contribute to the advancement of technology and society through quality education, cutting-edge research, and community engagement.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in computer education, research, and innovation, recognized globally for excellence and impact in shaping the future of technology and producing world-class graduates.
